Moonfall World Conquest

Choose your map and commanders, place reinforcements, roll dice, and conquer territory by territory.

Core rules

Reinforcements. Minimum 3 armies per turn, controlled territories divided by 3, plus complete-region bonuses.

Attack. Attack from an adjacent territory or a bridge-linked territory with at least 2 armies. The attacker rolls up to 3 dice, the defender up to 2; ties go to the defender.

Conquest. When defense reaches 0, the territory changes sides, armies advance, and the first conquest of the turn grants a card.
