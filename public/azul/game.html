Rules

On your turn, take all tiles of one color from one factory or from the center. If you take from a factory, every tile left on that factory moves to the center.

Place the selected tiles on one pattern line that accepts that color. A line accepts a color only if it is empty or already contains that color, and that wall row does not already contain that color. Overflow goes to the floor.

The first player marker is taken with the first center draft and goes to the floor. That player starts the next round.

When all factories and the center are empty, completed pattern lines move one tile to the wall. Score 1 point if isolated, otherwise score the connected horizontal and vertical lines created by the tile.

The floor line loses points from left to right: -1, -1, -2, -2, -2, -3, -3. Score never goes below 0.

The game ends after a player completes a horizontal wall row. End bonuses: +2 for each complete row, +7 for each complete column, +10 for each complete color set. Highest score wins; ties are broken by completed rows.

Each turn has 60 seconds. If a player does not move in time, they are eliminated and the opponent wins.
